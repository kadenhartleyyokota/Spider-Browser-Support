Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 19, 2025

Your Privacy Matters

Spider Browser is built with privacy at its core. We don't track you. We don't sell your data. Your browsing is yours alone.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • Your name (from your Google account)
  • A unique user ID

This information is used solely for authentication and to sync your data across devices.

1.2 Browsing Data

Spider Browser stores the following data to provide its core functionality:

  • Nodes: Web pages you've visited, their positions on your canvas, and connections between them
  • Bookmarks: URLs and titles of pages you've bookmarked, organized in folders
  • Browsing History: URLs and titles of pages you've visited (if history tracking is enabled in Settings)
  • Settings: Your preferences such as web zoom level and history tracking toggle

1.3 Local Data

The following data is stored locally on your device and never leaves your Mac:

  • Downloaded files
  • Web page screenshots (for node previews)
  • Cached web content
  • Cookies and website data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to:

  • Authenticate your account
  • Sync your browsing canvas, bookmarks, and history across your devices
  • Provide customer support when you contact us
  • Send important service updates (with your permission)

We do NOT:

  • Track your browsing behavior
  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Share your data with anyone except as required by law

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Cloud Storage

Your browsing data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) with:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Encryption at rest
  • Access restricted to your account only

3.2 Local Storage

Data stored locally on your Mac is protected by:

  • macOS App Sandbox (isolated from other apps)
  • FileVault encryption (if enabled on your Mac)
  • Core Data encryption with FileProtectionType.complete

4. Third-Party Services

Spider Browser uses the following third-party services:

4.1 Firebase (Google)

Used for:

  • User authentication
  • Cloud data sync

Firebase Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

4.2 Google Sign-In

Used for authentication. We only receive your email address and name.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access Your Data

You can view all your data within Spider Browser at any time.

5.2 Delete Your Data

You can delete:

  • Individual nodes, bookmarks, or history items within the app
  • Your entire account by contacting us at kadenhartleyyokota@gmail.com

5.3 Disable History Tracking

You can disable browsing history tracking in Settings at any time.

5.4 Export Your Data

Contact us at kadenhartleyyokota@gmail.com to request an export of your data.

6. Email Communications

We may email you for:

  • Important service updates
  • Security alerts
  • Feedback requests (during beta)
  • Bug reports and feature updates

You can opt out of non-essential emails by replying to any email with "unsubscribe" or contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

Spider Browser is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an email to your registered email address
  • Displaying a notice in the app

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at:

Email: kadenhartleyyokota@gmail.com

10. Legal Compliance

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests from authorities.
